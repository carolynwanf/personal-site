Coral Generator

Overview

when:

Last week of March 2021

Objective:

Translate the splendor of Blue Planet II onto the small small screen (i.e. make something cool inspired by the ocean).

my motivations:

I had just learned about procedural generation and I was curious about how it worked. I thought that coral would be a perfect thing to figure out how to procedurally generate because it looks like there's a set of rules that dictate how every species grows.

technologies:

React, Node.js, JS, seedrandom npm package.

Where can I see this?

Github and coralgenerator.herokuapp.com

Current solutions:

There are lots of cute ocean flash games out there. I like this one.

The final product:

A website that lets you randomly generate coral seascapes and download or save the seed of ones you like.
